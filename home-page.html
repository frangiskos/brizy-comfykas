COMFYKAS Cleaning and Renovation Services Ltd

Comfykas is active in the fields of cleaning and renovation of commercial and residential premises. We have qualified personnel, as well as the appropriate machinery and equipment to perform quality services. We offer services that include cleaning of premises, construction or maintenance of buildings, decoration and repair of apartments, offices and other facilities. In addition, the cleaning methods and products we use are environmentally friendly, with an emphasis on quality and customer satisfaction.
